About Us

Classic Stables is a premier racehorse breeding facility dedicated to excellence across Wales, England. We proudly manage a collection of 25 elite broodmares and 10 exceptional stallions, with approximately 20 foals born each year and 65 horses under training UK-wide. With locations in Cardiff, Newport, and Swansea, our stables provide a nurturing environment that fosters the growth of exceptional racehorses.

Our proud history includes the successful breeding of notable racehorses, with over 15 champions emerging from Classic Stables, leaving a lasting mark on the racing community. At Classic Stables, we are passionate about continuing our tradition of producing winning racehorses, dedicated to the integrity and spirit of equestrian excellence.
