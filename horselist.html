Our Stallions

Royal Heritage

Royal Heritage – A true legacy and pride of Classic Stables, Royal Heritage comes from a prestigious royal bloodline. He has won titles wherever he goes, including his latest victory in 2024. With his striking appearance and commanding presence, he is a stallion to behold and is highly sought after for breeding. In 2022, Heritage Star, sired by Royal Heritage, proudly secured 2nd place in a breeding showcase where Royal Heritage himself took 1st. This unforgettable display of his lineage's strength left a lasting impression on horse lovers. With an average covering rate of 5-6 mares per week, he continues to set a remarkable benchmark in the breeding arena.

Windrunner

Windrunner – Another pride of Classic Stables, Windrunner is a racing gem hailing from a distinguished lineage. He has made significant contributions to the equine industry, achieving multiple top 3 placements in regional competitions, including 1st place in the 2023 State Show Jumping Championship. With a covering rate of 4-5 mares per week, Windrunner has produced over 30 exceptional offspring, including Storm Chaser.

Thunderstrike

ThunderStrike – A magnificent bay stallion with unmatched power and grace, ThunderStrike cemented his legacy with a historic victory at the 2021 Royal Derby. Known for his speed and stamina, he continues to produce exceptional offspring, with 7 colts and 5 fillies making waves in show jumping and endurance racing. His most notable progeny, FireBlaze, is following closely in his footsteps, already securing two regional titles and proving to be a rising star in the equine world.

Nightshadow

Night Shadow – A prestigious dressage horse from the esteemed Hanoverian bloodline, Night Shadow has established himself as a standout competitor in the equine world. He has won several regional and national dressage shows, including the 2022 National Dressage Championship and the 2023 Regional Dressage Series. With a remarkable covering rate of 3-4 mares per week, Night Shadow has produced three exceptional progeny who are excelling in dressage training and making their mark in competitions.

Fireblaze

Fire Blaze – A striking bay stallion with an impressive athletic build, Fire Blaze has quickly established himself as a force in the equine world. As the standout progeny of ThunderStrike, he inherits not only his sire’s incredible speed and stamina but also his competitive spirit. With 2 colts and 1 filly already on the ground, Fire Blaze is making waves in both show jumping and endurance racing. His exceptional talent was showcased when he secured two regional titles in 2023, captivating audiences with his powerful performances and dynamic presence on the track.
